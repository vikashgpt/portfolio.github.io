About Us

Bharath TV Services was established with the sole motive of providing a reliable, perfection and good quality repairing service to the customers of Bangalore city. In today’s Indian market, every television manufacturer offers a 1 year warranty after which they charge more than the customer's expectation. Because of this, customers find private home services more affordable. Thus, Bharath services is established to provide a direct initiative service from our service center to our customers doorstep.
